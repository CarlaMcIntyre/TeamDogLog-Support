# TeamDog Log Privacy Policy

**Effective date: 18 July 2026**

TeamDog Log is a private record-keeping application for assistance dog, service dog, therapy dog and pet dog teams.

## Information we collect

TeamDog Log does not require an account and does not collect names, email addresses, location data or other personal information.

Information entered into TeamDog Log is stored locally on the user’s device. This may include dog profiles, training logs, public access records, incident logs, care records and weekly reviews.

The developer does not receive or have access to this information.

## Data storage and export

App records are stored locally on the user’s device. Users are responsible for protecting their device and any exported files.

If a user chooses to export their records, the app creates a file that the user can save or share using Apple’s standard sharing system. TeamDog Log does not automatically upload exported information.

Deleting the app may permanently remove locally stored records unless the user has separately retained an exported copy or device backup.

## In-app purchases

TeamDog Log offers an optional non-consumable in-app purchase called TeamDog Log Plus.

Purchases and restorations are processed securely by Apple through the App Store. The developer does not receive or store the user’s payment-card information.

## Notifications

TeamDog Log may request permission to provide local reminders. These notifications are created and managed on the user’s device. Notification permission can be changed through the device’s Settings app.

## Tracking and analytics

TeamDog Log does not use advertising, cross-app tracking or third-party analytics. The app does not sell or share personal information with advertisers or data brokers.

## Third-party services

Apple services may be used to process in-app purchases, provide device backups and display the standard sharing interface. Apple processes information according to its own privacy policies.

## Children’s privacy

TeamDog Log is not designed to collect personal information from children. Because the app does not operate user accounts or an online data-collection service, the developer does not knowingly collect children’s personal information.

## Changes to this policy

This privacy policy may be updated when the app’s features or data practices change. The latest version will be published on this page with its effective date.
